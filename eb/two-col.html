Elizabeth Knight is a long-time Houston resident who has been practicing psychotherapy for 35 years.

As a clinician and an educator, her areas of expertise are group dynamics, human sexuality and women's issues.

Elizabeth is a Distinguished Fellow of the American Group Psychotherapy Association and served as President from 2006-2008.

Elizabeth serves as an Assistant Professor, Department of Psychiatry Baylor College of Medicine, Houston.

She is also an Assistant Professor Department of Psychiatry at the University of Texas Health and Science Center at Houston.

After graduating Stanford with a degree in psychology, Elizabeth earned a Master's degree in Social Work from the University of Houston.
